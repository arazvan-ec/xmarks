## What changed

- **Delegation triggers in `/flywheel:work`** (adopted from gentle-ai): concrete,
  advisory thresholds for when to hand off to a fresh-context subagent instead of
  grinding solo and bloating context —
  - reading **4+ files** to understand an area → delegate exploration;
  - about to touch **2+ non-trivial files** → get a fresh-context review before advancing;
  - **~20 tool calls / ~5 exploratory reads** deep without converging → pause and re-plan.
- Guidance only — advisory, not enforced by a hook; no new commands, agents, or
  config surface. Also surfaced in the README and `/flywheel:help`. Installed repos
  pick it up on the next re-vendor; no action required.
